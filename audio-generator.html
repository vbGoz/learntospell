Word Club Audio Generator

Generate audio files for all 221 spelling words using your browser's text-to-speech.

Instructions:

  1. Select your preferred voice (Google US English recommended)
  2. Adjust speech rate if needed (0.7 is good for spelling)
  3. Click "Generate All Audio Files"
  4. Wait for all files to generate (takes 2-3 minutes)
  5. Click "Download All as ZIP"
  6. Extract the ZIP to your project's /audio folder
